Our traffic sign recognition project(Traeye) aims to enhance road safety by detecting both traffic signs and driver drowsiness in real-time. Using computer vision algorithms, the system identifies and interprets traffic signs along the road, providing timely alerts to the driver. Additionally, it incorporates drowsiness detection technology, monitoring the driver's behavior and alerting them if signs of drowsiness are detected, thereby preventing potential accidents due to driver fatigue.
